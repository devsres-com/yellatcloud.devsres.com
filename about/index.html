About One Man Datacenter

One Man Datacenter is the first of the Dev SREs Network Initiative, an attempt to document to the world the interesting challenges of a Sysadmin turned into SRE by the force of chance trying to earn the right to wear that title!

Here, we will describe our experience of building a virtual datacenter using bleeding edge open-source technology inside an extremely dated old fashion enterprise. Our main goal is to provide Kubernetes as a PaaS to our users, using all kinds of tools to make it real:

  • Calico as our CNI;
  • Ceph as our persistent storage;
  • Haproxy Ingress as our Ingress Controller
  • Prometheus and Grafana as our monitoring toolset;
  • Graylog as the output display tool for the users;
